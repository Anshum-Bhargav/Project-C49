Camel

Interesting Facts About camel

  • Camels are also called the ship of the desert.
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!

Chameleon

Interesting Facts About chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of iguana suborder.
  • They change color is to regulate their body temperatures.
  • Unlike many other lizards they can't shed and regrow their tails!

Pigeon

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • They are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • They also have excellent hearing abilities.
